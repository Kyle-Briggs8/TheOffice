# Art attribution

## Character & furniture sprites (`sprites/`)

From the "Free Office Pixel Art" pack by **arlantr** — "Free to use any way you want."

- itch.io: https://arlantr.itch.io/
- OpenGameArt: https://opengameart.org/users/arlantr

Original files live in `free-office-pixel-art/` at the repo root; the copies in
`sprites/` are renamed only (julia-* are the Julia walk-cycle sheets, 4 frames
of 64x64 per direction).

## Tileset (`maps/office.json` → "office-tiles")

The floor/wall/carpet tileset is generated at runtime by
`client/src/scenes/OfficeScene.ts` (`makeTilesetTexture`) — no image asset.
The Tiled JSON map itself is generated by `client/scripts/genMap.mjs`.
